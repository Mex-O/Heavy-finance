HEAVY FINANCE

Reflect token on the Binance Smart Chain.

Heavy dosen't aim to replace any existing tokens or coins. The mission of Heavy is to help more people recognize and understand a blockchain, explore oppurtunities for blockchain, enable more people experience the crypto world. HVY is a deflation currency. Total maximum supply of tokens is limited to 200,000 HVY, and all of them are already issued. No more HVY tokens will ever exist. HVY is a community-based experimental project, which was prepared for the purpose of deflation and was produced on the Binance Smart Chain. The Heavy token smart contract is designed to burn 1% per transfer.

How to claim HVY

Download Metamask on Google Chrome if you don't have it, and load the wallet into it. Then click on "CONNECT WALLET" above. Once your wallet is connected, you will be able to claim. If you do, you can cliick the "CLAIM" button. Make sure you set the transaction gas high enough ("Fast" is preffered), and submit your claim transaction.
After claiming your HEAVY, add the contract to your wallet to see your tokens. In METAMASK click on "Add Token" and fill in the contract address listed below. The rest of the information should autofill, and the tokens should be visible.

OUR MISSION

Heavy token is a community-based experimental project, which was created as a deflationary currency and produced on the Binance Smart Chain

After claiming, since Heavy is a community project, the community will decide how the Heavy token will develop

The information provided about the development of the project is published monthly by the Heavy team.

The intention is not to be used for day-to-day transactions, but rather as a decentralized hedge against traditional inflationary instrument.

We have a devoted team of staff members with years of experience working at many of the other conventions in the area.
